# HW11b_Proof_by_Contradiction

### Problem 1

Use resolution to prove:

$$ \lnot P \lor R $$
$$ \lnot Q \lor R $$
$$ P \lor Q $$
$$-----$$
$$ ∴ R $$

Prove by contradiction, using resolution.

### Problem 2

Use resolution to prove:

$$ \lnot Q $$
$$ R $$
$$-----$$
$$ ∴ \lnot P $$

Prove by contradiction, using resolution.
